About Us

Cool Cats Cafe is the place to relax after a long day. Spend time with our cats, then treat yourself to a drink or snack. We also have board games and books for visitors to use.

All of our cats are rescues and ready to be adopted to a loving home. Fill out our adoption application to begin the process.
